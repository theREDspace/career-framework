---
code: SQE1
position: Senior QE Specialist
tier: sqe
theme: Masters
description: Demonstrating deep expertise and leading complex testing initiatives
order: 2
competencies:
  technology:
    keyword: Masters
    summary: Demonstrates deep understanding and expertise in multiple technologies and frameworks.
    bullets:
      - Leads complex testing activities across multiple mediums with consistently meaningful results
      - Requires minimal oversight and support, proactively identifies and resolves issues with broad impact
      - Significantly reduces turnaround time for testing activities while maintaining high quality
      - Demonstrates mastery of multiple frameworks and programming languages
      - Consistently adds complex tests and is reliable for pull request reviews
      - Able to adapt quickly to the latest tech trends (e.g., cloud-based testing tools) and implement them for projects
      - Can troubleshoot complex integration issues across platforms
  solution:
    keyword: Owns
    summary: Proactively seeks out and implements new tools and methodologies to improve testing processes.
    bullets:
      - Continuously learns and applies new skills, shares knowledge with the team
      - Increases team velocity and quality through innovative testing strategies
      - Regularly reviews and improves code changes for comprehensive understanding and scoping
      - Creates testing strategies that reduce redundancy, cover edge cases, and optimize the use of resources
      - Solves issues by collaborating closely with development and DevOps teams, understanding the entire ecosystem of a product, and coming up with testing approaches that fit within those parameters
  people:
    keyword: Mentors
    summary: Identifies and addresses gaps in requirements or processes with innovative solutions.
    bullets:
      - Asks insightful questions about features and risks, improving overall productivity
      - Completes complex tasks within deadlines, mentors others
      - Demonstrates deep curiosity and understanding of issues, leading to innovative solutions
      - Expert in automation across multiple frameworks and platforms
      - Regularly takes initiative to mentor junior QEs
      - Provides meaningful feedback in code reviews, helping others to grow
      - Encourages collaboration within the team to improve overall quality
      - Acts as a go-to person for junior staff, helping them to understand not just how but why things are done
      - Encourages continuous learning by setting up knowledge-sharing sessions or technical workshops
  process:
    keyword: Challenges
    summary: Challenges the team processes, seeking ways to improve them.
    bullets:
      - Raises constructive feedback with regards to project process
      - Actively contributes during Project Retros to help shape and improve the process
      - Understands the impact of feedback, and when to provide it in an effective and productive way
      - Contributes regularly to improving the process flow and is able to explain it to new people on the project
      - Can onboard people onto projects or the company
      - Finds gaps in testing or process and sees how the team could better utilize existing tools
      - Streamlines test execution by integrating automated test suites within CI/CD pipelines
      - Implements best practices and refines documentation for the whole team to follow, ensuring consistency
  influence:
    keyword: Multiple Teams
    summary: Establishes and follows best practices for the project, ensuring high standards.
    bullets:
      - Proactively identifies and addresses blockers, ensuring smooth project progression
      - Adapts processes to fit project needs, ensuring efficiency and effectiveness
      - Calls out blockers and issues promptly, creates detailed bug tickets and PRs
      - Works cross-functionally to impact projects in different areas of the organization
      - Offers insights that influence feature development and testing timelines, ensuring that quality considerations are part of the initial planning
      - Proactively leads discussions on process improvements, attends cross-team meetings, and makes recommendations on overall quality improvements
  client:
    keyword: Autonomy
    summary: Proactively identifies quality risks and engineers testability into features.
    bullets:
      - Doesn't just automate tests; asks clarifying questions during grooming that expose missing requirements, edge cases, and testability gaps before development begins
      - Takes ambiguous business requests and engineers quality validation approaches that prove the feature delivers real value, saving the client time and money
      - Proactively identifies technical dependencies, integration points, and architectural decisions that could impact quality early in the process
      - Offers technical suggestions to improve testability, observability, and error handling while staying within the original scope
      - Can be given a high-level goal and is trusted to return with a comprehensive quality engineering strategy that covers automation, monitoring, and risk mitigation
