---
code: QE1
position: QE Specialist
tier: qe
theme: Specializes
description: Focusing on technologies and building reliable test automation
order: 0
competencies:
  technology:
    keyword: Specializes
    summary: Focusing on one or more technologies and takes initiative to learn new ones.
    bullets:
      - Carries out testing activities for a given medium with predictably meaningful results
      - Needs limited oversight and support for typical testing duties and begins to recognize patterns in issues that can carry a broader than anticipated impact
      - Improved turnaround time for testing activities
      - Demonstrates foundational programming knowledge while actively learning required project frameworks
      - Demonstrates ongoing growth in writing complext tests, building the consistency needed to independently handle pull request reviews
      - Is curious and inquisitive with their teams and the technologies they use
      - Has proven to be capable of learning and retaining skills and context. Shows that they learn from mistakes and doesn't require specific feedback and instruction to be repeatedly delivered
      - Continues to seek new ways to contribute to their team or context
      - Begins to look at code changes connected to the work they're testing for more comprehensive understanding and scoping
  solution:
    keyword: Enhances
    summary: Successfully identifies gaps in requirements or processes.
    bullets:
      - Develops their productivity skills by learning how to ask meaningful questions about features and what risks seem visible
      - Completes assigned tasks in agreed-upon deadlines, asks for help when needed
      - Demonstrates curiosity around oddities or issues
      - Developing their automation skills in a specific framework and platform
      - Reaches out to seniors when there are issues with their code, researches a little before hand
  people:
    keyword: Learns
    summary: Quickly learns from others and steps up as needed.
    bullets:
      - Developing the ability to effectively communicate within a team and with clients
      - Does some research on their own before reaching out to seniors
      - Able to complete basic automation scripts independently
      - Balances independent solution research with recognizing a wall that requires senior outreach
  process:
    keyword: Follows
    summary: Follows the process laid out for the project.
    bullets:
      - Learning to call out blockers and issues in timely manner
      - Learning which the proper process is, and that processes can be different depending on the project
      - Calls out blockers to the channel and team
      - Creates bug tickets and PRs following the teams preference
      - Follows the teams processes on meetings / build and testing cycles, etc
  influence:
    keyword: Subsystem
    summary: Makes an individual impact on a team or project.
    bullets:
      - Relies on team in a positive way, works towards asking well articulated and informed questions
      - Team player, contributes to broader project team, also showcasing independence and ability to work on own
      - Gives feedback on tickets, features, bugs, and PRs
      - While learning the project, framework and features asks questions about edge cases and rainy day scenarios based on information provided in previous conversations which can drive the development to alter course for the better
  client:
    keyword: Reliability
    summary: Builds reliable automated tests that provide confidence in releases.
    bullets:
      - Ensures that automated tests align with the acceptance criteria (AC) and validate expected behavior
      - Shows a commitment to quality by writing maintainable, readable test code that other engineers can understand and extend
      - Represents REDspace well in internal meetings by being prepared, punctual, and attentive to project goals
      - Understands that even small test automation scripts contribute to the overall quality safety net for the client's product
      - Prioritizes test reliability and accuracy over rushing to increase test count
---
