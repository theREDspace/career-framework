---
code: SD3
position: Software Developer
tier: sd
theme: Trust
description: Trusted expert who proactively reduces risk
order: 2
competencies:
  technology:
    keyword: Master
    summary: Go-to expert for team-specific tech; unblocks peers.
    bullets:
      - "Trusted technical reviewer: serves as a high-integrity reviewer for their area of specialization"
      - Deepens their understanding of the technology stack and the dependencies within
      - Deepens knowledge in several areas of development, moving beyond a single language or framework
      - Demonstrates technical fluency by identifying and clarifying technical risks up front
      - Maintains high standards for code quality and consistency across the project
  solution:
    keyword: Solidifies
    summary: Handles "clarification up front" to reduce requirement gaps.
    bullets:
      - Takes high-level business requirements and proactively identifies the sequence of technical steps needed to achieve the goal
      - Proactively identifies missing edge cases and technical risks during the "Discovery" phase, before coding begins
      - Understands and asks the right questions at the start of a project to reduce requirement gaps
      - Ensures the component or subsystem is "Solid" by accounting for how it might fail (e.g., error handling, input validation)
      - Is able to debug issues to the root problem and not just provide fix for the symptoms and explain why it occurred
      - Able to handle the "how" of connecting multiple components or services to ensure a seamless flow
  people:
    keyword: Empowers
    summary: Proactively identifies peer struggles and offers help.
    bullets:
      - Synthesizes information across the team to reduce confusion and suggests improvements to how the team communicates
      - Takes the lead in bringing new developers up to speed on the project, team norms, and culture
      - Explains technical decisions and tradeoffs to stakeholders (Product, Client) in a way that builds trust
      - Leads internal meetings to share knowledge and align the team on new approaches
  process:
    keyword: Adjuster
    summary: Identifies friction and suggests improvements.
    bullets:
      - Suggests ways to reduce the way "waste" or "friction" occurs within the development lifecycle
      - Recognizes that different projects might require different process weights and adjusts their approach accordingly
      - Actively contributes to (or leads) Retrospectives, moving beyond identifying problems to proposing actionable solutions
      - Brings in new "best practice" workflows from the broader industry or other REDspace teams to improve their immediate team's velocity
  influence:
    keyword: Initiates
    summary: Spots problems and proposes small fixes independently.
    bullets:
      - Impacts multiple teams by suggesting and initiating improvements that have a broader reach than a single project
      - Identifies opportunities for technical or process improvements and takes the initiative to socialise them with peers and leadership
      - Acts as a bridge between teams, sharing best practices and ensuring that technical "silos" are broken down
      - Presents to the company or specific groups, sharing knowledge or project successes to elevate the collective understanding
      - Synthesizes information across the team to reduce confusion, proactively clarifying goals when they see a peer struggling
      - Contributes to company-wide technical health by participating in guilds, documentation audits, or cross-team knowledge sharing
  client:
    keyword: Autonomy
    summary: Proactively clarifies requirements up front.
    bullets:
      - Doesn’t just start coding; they ask the clarifying questions during grooming that expose missing requirements before development begins
      - Takes ambiguous business requests and translates them into clear technical tasks, saving the client time and money
      - Proactively identifies dependencies with other teams or third-party vendors early in the process
      - Offers small, immediate suggestions to improve the user experience or technical efficiency while staying within the original scope
      - Can be given a high-level goal and is trusted to return with a plan that covers all the bases
---
