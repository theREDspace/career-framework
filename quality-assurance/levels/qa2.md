---
code: QA2
position: QA Specialist
tier: qa
theme: Specializes
description: Deepening skills and delivering independently
order: 1
competencies:
  technology:
    keyword: Specializes
    summary: Focusing on one or more technologies and takes initiative to learn new ones.
    bullets:
      - Carries out testing activities for a given medium with predictably meaningful results
      - Needs limited oversight and support for typical testing duties and begins to recognize patterns in issues that can carry a broader than anticipated impact
      - Expands foothold in a given platform or technology, approaching more of a Subject Matter Expertise
      - Improved turnaround time for testing activities
      - Can work on various project types with some guidance
      - Seeks new ways to contribute to their team or context
      - Begins to look at code changes connected to the work they're testing for more comprehensive understanding and scoping
  solution:
    keyword: Designs
    summary: Designs and implements documentation and test cases/passes for full features and edge cases.
    bullets:
      - Capable of prioritizing tasks and able to identify what needs tested vs what does not
      - Works independently, resourceful and displays initiative
      - Ensures bugs are documented properly and identifies the possible causes
      - Executes assigned tasks independently, requiring minimal guidance or supervision to achieve objectives
      - Acts as a reliable resource for peers, readily collaborating to ensure the team's collective success
      - Creates test cases and light documentation for testing features
      - Creates bug ticket, assigns ticket to developer, and speaks to PM/Dev about the issue discovered
  people:
    keyword: Supports
    summary: Proactively supports other team members and helps them to be successful.
    bullets:
      - Demonstrates proactivity in problem solving
      - Understands how their role fits into the larger project
      - Calls out risks in requirements
      - Troubleshoots bugs, seeks potential causes and provides developers a better starting point for investigation
      - During planning meetings asks important questions about how rainy day and sunny day scenarios will be handled
  process:
    keyword: Enforces
    summary: Enforces the team processes, making sure everybody understands the benefits and tradeoffs.
    bullets:
      - Comfortable speaking up in projects, calls out when the process is not being followed in a respectful productive way, suggests ways to improve the process
      - Familiarity with multiple flows and is able to work on multiple process types (Waterfall, Agile, Scrum, etc)
      - Demonstrates commitment to process clarity by addressing gaps in ticketing and documentation, driving improvements for Dev and QA workflow
  influence:
    keyword: Team
    summary: Makes an impact on the whole team rather than just parts.
    bullets:
      - Elevates team-wide standards by identifying deep-rooted bugs and driving their resolution
      - Demonstrates a high level of independence, leading by example and inspiring greater self-sufficiency in peers
      - Demonstrates a high level of communication of assurance
      - When testing, able to communicate effectively amongst the team of how something was tested and what was found, and possible root cause analysis
      - Demonstrates strong influence by identifying edges cases and clearly communicating the 'why' behind the testing
  client:
    keyword: Predictability
    summary: Consistent quality coverage with few escapes.
    bullets:
      - Delivers test coverage that is thorough enough to prevent defects from reaching production
      - Provides a consistent pace of testing that allows Project Managers to give clients accurate timelines
      - Understands how changes in one area can impact the broader product, ensuring comprehensive regression coverage
      - Communicates as soon as they realize testing will take longer than anticipated, preventing "last-minute" surprises for the client
      - Ensures the standard user journey is bulletproof, providing immediate and obvious value to the client's end users
---
