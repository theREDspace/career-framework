  technology:

    bullets:
      - Understands project admin and flow (Jira, project management, estimations, PR flow, flow from pitch to deployment)
      - Can effectively guide direct reports in their technical career growth, even if those individuals have specialized experience in excess of your own
      - General knowledge of overall architecture of websites/apps, mobile apps, set top boxes
      - Can multitask in mentoring and completing project work
  solution:
    keyword: Enhances & Designs
    summary: Identifies resource needs within the team and participates in external recruitment when required.
    bullets:
      - Swiftly manages resource needs within the team and participates in the external recruitment process when required
      - Identifies when automation is required, what type, where, and is able to determine the proper path to take
      - Notifies management of any challenges or roadblocks for direct reports
  people:
    keyword: Learns & Supports
    summary: Has direct reports, provides feedback and mentorship, and conducts performance reviews.
    bullets:
      - Strong team leadership skills and ability to mentor and coach junior team members
      - Advancing their skills as a lead, learning soft skills and how to employ them to be more effective leaders
      - Knowledge of quality assurance tools, ticketing software, and project management tools such as Jira, as well as planning their direct reports' time
      - Identifies when a direct report needs mentoring and coaches them
      - Knows the difference between "talking to" and "coaching/mentoring"
      - Continually trying to better themselves so they can better their direct reports
      - Knows what learning type their direct reports are and how to have effective conversations with them
  process:
    keyword: Follows & Enforces
    summary: Regularly checks in with direct reports and sends reports to management.
    bullets:
      - Consistently meets with direct reports interval, maintaining referrable documentation of the meeting and action items
      - Knows the process of various methodologies and can use any or swap to any as required (Scrum, Agile, Waterfall, etc)
      - Enforces the best practices with direct reports
      - Identifies what process is required and can shift between those effortlessly
  influence:
    keyword: Subsystem & Team
    summary: Able to drive a team of testers with insight into the project and platform.
    bullets:
      - Estimates effort on projects with various skill levels with minimal insight
      - Participates in hiring process and candidate evaluation
  client:
    keyword: Ownership
    summary: Aligns quality strategy with client's long-term goals.
    bullets:
      - Makes quality infrastructure decisions that account for where the client's business is going in 18–24 months, preventing expensive test debt later
      - Knows when it's appropriate to reduce test scope for a marketing launch and when it's critical to enforce full regression for stability
      - Understands how their project fits into the client's larger ecosystem and ensures quality coverage accounts for integration points
      - Proactively identifies security, compliance, or scalability concerns that could hurt the client's brand in the future
