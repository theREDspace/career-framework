---
code: SQA2
position: Senior QA Specialist
tier: sqa
theme: Masters
description: Leading quality strategy and elevating team capabilities
order: 3
competencies:
  technology:
    keyword: Masters
    summary: Has strong knowledge about the technology stack of the Software Under Test.
    bullets:
      - Able to effectively conduct root cause analysis
      - Familiar with a variety of test automation solutions and their purpose
      - Working knowledge of at least one of these solutions
      - Can be placed on a multitude of projects with little to no context or help and work effectively
      - Easily navigates a post-mortem for issues that get missed, and provides ownership/solutions for how they will be prevented in the future
      - Makes meaningful contributions to improving squad velocity through analysis of team process and cycle time
      - Can provide feedback for how and where best to apply automated testing strategies; has a solid understanding of DAMP coding principles
      - Able to provide support in analyzing what refactoring may be required to make code more testable
  solution:
    keyword: Evolves
    summary: Evolves the testing, documentation, and conversations of test.
    bullets:
      - Diligently works to make accurate estimates and deliver on those estimates
      - Recognized as a major contributor to projects
      - Regularly investigates bugs or possible bugs
      - Works alongside developers when troubleshooting bugs and solves the problems together
      - Creates and manages test suites and advises others on how to expand/combine tests to make them more efficient
      - Creates and manages test documentation for a project/feature and creates walkthroughs for features
      - Contributes in requirements meetings with valuable feedback and can demo features during the meetings effectively
      - Discovers bugs on platforms that are not their own, and reports them to the appropriate team
  people:
    keyword: Coordinates
    summary: Coordinates team members providing effective feedback and moderating discussions.
    bullets:
      - Makes others better through documentation, technical guidance, and mentoring or serving as a Test Lead on a project
      - Effectively delegates tasks and multiplies the effectiveness of others
      - Helps others understand the features, known bugs/gotchas, and test procedures
      - Listens and guides debates to help reach a consensus; once a decision is made, clearly communicates and backs that decision
      - Demonstrates strong comunication skills by clearly detailing the scope and risk factors of future releases to ensure successful delivery
      - Knows the strengths of their testers and delegates specific tasks to them
      - Creates testing walkthroughs for others to use (Developers, QA, PMs, etc)
      - Overall credibility with staff peers is be well established and sought out
  process:
    keyword: Adjusts
    summary: Adjusts the team processes, listening to feedback and guiding the team through the changes.
    bullets:
      - Takes initiative to identify and solve important problems, coordinating with others on technical issues
      - Adjusts team processes to improve overall efficiency
      - Helps others discover what the root issue is of a bug that is not their own
      - When a bug is discovered, investigates to discover which part of the project the bug is on and whom it should properly go to (Front-End, Back-End, API, etc), and works with that team to get the bug properly recorded and triaged
      - Thinks critically and creatively about when a process no longer serves the cross-disciplinary team
      - Can call out meaningful Agile ceremonies that are missing from the team, introduce new processes, or reduce the frequency of ceremonies the team doesn't benefit from, with specific and convincing explanations
  influence:
    keyword: Multiple Teams
    summary: Makes an impact not only on the whole team but also on other teams.
    bullets:
      - Shares knowledge with other team members and peers
      - Leads other testers, sometimes seniors, by example
      - Redirects potentially misguided efforts from others upon observation due to similar experiences they've learned from in the past
      - Is known to create and improve processes and write documentation that others use
      - Shares useful documentation outside of the specific project they're working on to Developers, PMs and other QA
      - Demonstrates strong influence by offering experience and guidance when discussions lack direction or include incorrect conclusions
  client:
    keyword: Trust
    summary: Acts as a quality strategist; offers risk-based tradeoffs.
    bullets:
      - Doesn't just say "pass" or "fail." Explains the quality risk and business impact of different paths, helping stakeholders make informed release decisions
      - Proactively suggests ways to achieve the same quality confidence with less testing overhead through smart risk assessment
      - Understands the client's business pressures (deadlines, budget cycles, stakeholders) and aligns quality strategy to support them
      - Capable and trusted to lead quality discussions with the client, maintaining a calm and professional demeanor even when discussing escaped defects or delays
---
