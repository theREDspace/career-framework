---
code: QA1
position: QA Specialist
tier: qa
theme: Adopts
description: Learning the fundamentals and building good habits
order: 0
competencies:
  technology:
    keyword: Adopts
    summary: Actively learns and adopts techniques, tools, and technology defined by the team.
    bullets:
      - Demonstrates a practical understanding of testing basics, utilizing assistive tools like browser devtools to inspect and verify software functionality
      - Focuses on growing their menu of capabilities whenever the opportunity arises
      - Demonstrates eagerness to learn and improve
      - Asks questions, looks for opportunities to shadow with colleagues and learn how best to contribute to their team
  solution:
    keyword: Enhances
    summary: Successfully identifies gaps in requirements or processes, and can test with given information or steps.
    bullets:
      - Able to write basic test cases and documentation
      - Develops their productivity skills by learning how to ask meaningful questions about features and what risks seem visible
      - Completes assigned tasks in agreed-upon deadlines; communicates changes to deadline expectations, and asks for help when needed
      - Demonstrates curiosity around oddities or issues
      - Sees the flows through multiple lenses enabling risks to become visible
      - Sees a bug and confirms the issue with senior QA and/or developers - asks questions about the bug
  people:
    keyword: Learns
    summary: Highly receptive to feedback and coaching, reviews assigned documentation, and is able to follow and execute assigned tests delegated to them.
    bullets:
      - Developing the ability to effectively communicate within a team and with clients and distinguish the team's roles
      - Exhibits company/team values and strives to understand and support them
      - Takes constructive feedback well and is driven to continuously learn
      - When given a document to follow is able to follow it with minor questions
  process:
    keyword: Follows
    summary: Follows the process laid out for the project.
    bullets:
      - Learning to call out blockers and issues in a timely manner
      - Learning which the proper process is, and that processes can be different depending on the project
      - Calls out blockers to the channel and team
      - Creates bug tickets following the team's preference
      - Follows the team's processes on meetings, build and testing cycles, etc
  influence:
    keyword: Subsystem
    summary: Makes an individual impact on a team or project.
    bullets:
      - Relies on team in a positive way, works towards asking well articulated and informed questions
      - Team player, contributes to broader project team, also showcasing independence and ability to work on own
      - Gives feedback on tickets, features, and bugs
      - While learning the project and features asks questions about edge cases and rainy day scenarios based on information provided in previous conversations which can drive the development to alter course for the better
  client:
    keyword: Reliability
    summary: Delivers thorough and accurate testing.
    bullets:
      - Ensures that test execution aligns with the acceptance criteria (AC) provided in the ticket
      - Shows a commitment to quality by double-checking their findings before reporting, reducing noise in the bug tracking process
      - Represents REDspace well in internal meetings by being prepared, punctual, and attentive to project goals
      - Understands that even testing small bug fixes or minor features contributes to the overall health of the client's product
      - Prioritizes thoroughness and accuracy in testing over rushing through test passes
---
